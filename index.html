Features

Everything you need to make screenshots shine

One menu bar app that replaces three separate tools.

Screenshot Beautification

Wrap any screenshot in gradients, solid colors, or your own image. Adjust padding, corner radius, shadow, and frame color.

Code Screenshots

Paste a snippet, pick a language and theme. 21 languages, 10 themes — Dracula, Monokai, Nord, GitHub and more. Export PNG or SVG.

Annotations

Arrows, rectangles, ellipses, text, freehand, markers, numbered counters, ruler. Built for tutorials and bug reports.

Auto-Beautify

One click to auto-pick a matching gradient, padding, shadow, and frame based on your screenshot's dominant colors.

Privacy & Auto-Redaction

Auto-detects faces, emails, API keys, and phone numbers and redacts them before you share. All on-device — nothing leaves your Mac.

Menu Bar & Hotkeys

Lives quietly in the menu bar. Capture any area or window with a global hotkey. Always one keypress away.
